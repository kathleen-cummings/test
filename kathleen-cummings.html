About me

Hi, I'm Kathleen — I make data work for the people who need it most

I'm a senior data analyst with 5+ years at CBRE, one of the world's largest commercial real estate firms. I specialize in data visualization, analytics automation, and product management — helping teams move faster and decide smarter.

My work spans client-facing Tableau dashboards that shape strategic decisions, to internal platform development on GeoNavigator, to building data quality protocols that protect the integrity of top-tier client deliverables.

Outside the office, I'm an endurance athlete — I've completed multiple marathons, the Death Ride Tour of the California Alps, and Ironman 70.3 Santa Cruz. I bring that same discipline and drive to everything I do.
